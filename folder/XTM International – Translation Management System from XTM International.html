Translation Management Solution

Localizing and deploying material across multiple regions is complex. You need a translation solution that is simple, scalable and robust. XTM translation management system (TMS) will help you deliver your content localized into multiple languages faster and more cost-effectively. Discover XTM

 

  • Life Science

    Built to meet the demanding regulatory requirements of life science companies, XTM is a secure and powerful translation management solution. Easy to set up and use.

  • Gaming

    Simultaneously release your titles in all languages. Localize in-game content with XTM to gain full control over quality, budgets, assets, suppliers and deadlines.

  • Automotive

    Optimize the translation of automotive content by deploying agile localization technology to dramatically reduce translation costs and keep control of the translation process.

  • Marketing

    Localize marketing content quickly and easily. Manage transcreation or review marketing material in-context with XTM Visual Editor linked directly to your CMS.

  • Education

    Manage and control the translation of your global content with XTM. XTM is a secure and scalable enterprise translation management solution.

 

XTM Connectors

Send content to XTM for translation directly from your CMS or other system. Use automated translation workflows, machine translation, and post editing to reduce errors, shorten turnaround time, and eliminate repetitive work.
